Workshop details

Introduction to 1.44 & 2.44

AS91074 and AS91371

Workshop description

This workshop is aimed for educators planning on teaching Computer Science at NCEA level 1 & 2. This workshop starts with an introduction to the topic then splits into two groups.

For those who have never used the level 1 Computer Science standard before (1.44/AS91074) Caitlin will go over the purpose of this standard, investigate the topic, show some examples of how to get students engaged with the ideas in it, and cover the teacher assessment guides. Recommended for first timers.

For those who have had an introduction to Computer Science and wish to cover level 1 and 2 (1.44/AS91074 and 2.44/AS91371) at a faster pace over the three days. Tim will cover the content, provide steps on how to teach concepts, and introduce the assessment guides.

This workshop is recommended for those who have never taught Computer Science before to those who want a refresher on levels 1 & 2.

Workshop leaders

Led by Tim Bell and Cailtin Duncan

Hands on practice with 1.44, 2.44, or 3.44

Workshop description

Already know a bit about 1.44, 2.44, or 3.44 and want to jump in with some hands on practice?

This workshop will allow you to pick one of the 3 CS external standards (1.44, 2.44, or 3.44), and work in a group to carry out relevant activities, and then write a report on it. Most of the workshop will consist of working in groups with tutor help, although there will be a general presentation on writing good reports, avoiding plagarism, and optional presentations on teaching 3.44.

Prerequisite knowledge

  • To focus on 1.44, you will need to be familiar with the 1.44 content.
  • To focus on 2.44, you will need to be familiar with the 2.44 content.
  • To focus on 3.44, you will need to have confidently taught 1.44 or 2.44 or 3.44, no prior 3.44 experience is required.

Workshop leader

Led by Heidi Newton
