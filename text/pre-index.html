Welcome!

Welcome to the website for the Christchurch Computer Science for High Schools conference, at the University of Canterbury in December 2016. Our conference is focused on preparing for the computer science and programming achievement standards for NCEA. Full details regarding the conference's timetable, location, etc will be announced mid year. We have begun organising funding for the event, and planning to run it over five days in late November 2016. The first two days will be optional programming workshop, with the main conference running over last three days (as 2015 was scheduled). We expect to confirm the conference by the end of May 2016.

Conference in 2015

The conference aims to cover:

  • Computer Science NCEA Standards (both introductions and analysis)
  • Programming NCEA Standards (both introductions and analysis)
  • Student stories and industry visit
  • Networking opportunities with other NZ educators
