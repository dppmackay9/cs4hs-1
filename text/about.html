About

There are two independent CS4HS workshops running throughout New Zealand in 2016. Our conference in Christchurch is focused on the Computer Science and Programming Achievement standards, for teachers who are at beginner/intermediate level, so the discussions will be more focussed on getting started.

The focus of the Victoria University of Wellington CS4HS workshop will be to provide more advanced programming, algorithmics, computational thinking and robotics professional development to New Zealand high school teachers, and create take-away resources that they can apply to encourage and challenge their mid-range and excellent students at years 11-13.

The events are independent and are covering different areas, so it's fine to apply to go to more than one (if you have the time!).
