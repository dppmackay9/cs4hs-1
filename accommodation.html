Accommodation

For those travelling to the conference from outside of Christchurch, accomodation is provided at University Hall.

If you are arriving at the beginning of a day, please do not check into your accommodation but come straight to the conference. We will look after your bags in a secure room and have them transported to the hall for you to pickup in the evening.
